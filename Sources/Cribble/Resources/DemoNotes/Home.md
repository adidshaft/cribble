# Welcome to Cribble

Cribble is a fast, native Markdown reader for your notes — with highlights, reading bookmarks, wiki links, on-device semantic search, and more. This bundled library is a living tutorial: **open any note below and learn a feature by using it.**

## Tips while you explore

- Select text and press **H** to highlight; **B** drops a reading bookmark.
- Press **P** to see your Reading Trail; `⌥⌘O` toggles the outline.
- Right-click a folder to **Pin** it to the top of the sidebar.
